# 8. Founder Story Library

## Purpose
Create a reusable inventory of stories for videos, posts, speeches, podcasts, interviews, and essays.

## Story 1: The Shawarma Margin
- Theme: entrepreneurial awakening
- Core moment: comparing production cost with retail price
- Lesson: opportunity often hides inside ordinary activities
- Signature line: “My first business started with a question about margin.”

## Story 2: My Father Taught Me to Pay My Friends
- Theme: leadership and fairness
- Lesson: people should be compensated even when a business is young

## Story 3: The Business Customers Loved but Systems Could Not Carry
- Theme: operations
- Lesson: demand is not enough
- Signature line: “Founder energy launched it. Weak systems limited it.”

## Story 4: The Damaged Stand
- Theme: infrastructure and resilience
- Lesson: businesses must prepare for operational shocks

## Story 5: The Four Delivery Bikes
- Theme: execution risk
- Lesson: growth plans require verification and control

## Story 6: Managing Other People's Money
- Theme: responsibility and failure
- Boundary: use only when emotionally and strategically ready
- Lesson: confidence cannot replace risk management and documentation

## Story 7: Moving to Canada
- Theme: reinvention
- Lesson: external systems do not always create the path expected

## Story 8: Discovering AI
- Theme: leverage
- Signature line: “AI did not give me an identity. It gave my ideas more leverage.”

## Story 9: Building Creed Consult
- Theme: mission
- Lesson: ideas need architecture, brands, systems, and execution

## Story 10: Building Volatile
- Theme: product development
- Lesson: lived problems and expertise can become products

## Story 11: The Chick-fil-A Walk-Away Lesson
- Theme: boundaries and self-leadership
- Lesson: small decisions reveal alignment under pressure

## Story 12: The Version of Me Holding Me Back
- Theme: identity
- Lesson: the problem may not be motivation; it may be the operating system

## Story 13: Becoming the Evidence
- Theme: transformation
- Lesson: the founder must become proof of the principles he teaches

## Story 14: Africa's People Are the Opportunity
- Theme: Africa and diaspora
- Lesson: talent needs bridges of trust, knowledge, capital, and systems

## Story safety levels

### Green
Food business, father’s lessons, AI discovery, moving countries, building Creed, learning video creation, fitness.

### Yellow
Business losses, delivery-bike loss, financial insecurity, relationship with money, job-search disappointment.

### Red
Identifiable trading clients, private relationships, legal matters, confidential third-party information.

## Story template
- Title
- Situation
- Tension
- Decision
- Outcome
- Lesson
- Brand pillar
- Safety level
- Evidence
- Best format
- Signature line
