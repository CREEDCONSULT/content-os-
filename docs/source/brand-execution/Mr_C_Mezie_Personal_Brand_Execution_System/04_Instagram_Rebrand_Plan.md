# 4. Instagram Rebrand Plan

## Objective
Transform the existing Instagram account into the human and visual home of the Mr. C. Mezie brand.

## Account decision
Rebrand the existing account unless historical content or audience quality creates serious strategic conflict.

## Audit categories

### Keep
Strong personal photos, sports, culture, food, style, thoughtful content, founder-history material.

### Archive
Posts that strongly conflict, low-quality clutter, outdated identity, irrelevant memes, repeated weak content.

### Reuse
Old business images, project history, fitness progress, travel, cultural material, food-business evidence.

## Profile reset

### Name field
Mr. C. Mezie | Builder Intelligence

### Bio
Building intelligent businesses, systems & ownership.  
AI • Entrepreneurship • Markets • Leadership  
Documenting the journey, block by block.  
↓ Build with me

### Profile image
Clean headshot, direct eye contact, neutral background, strong contrast, simple wardrobe.

## Pinned post 1: Reintroduction
Explain who Mezie is becoming, why the page is changing, what he has built, and what the audience can expect.

## Pinned post 2: What I Build
Show Creed Consult, Creed AI, Volatile, business systems, brand systems, and research.

## Pinned post 3: What This Page Helps You Do
Promise clarity around AI, systems, business, ownership, markets, and self-leadership.

## Grid logic
Use a flexible rhythm of human portrait, framework, project proof, Builder Walk, lifestyle, and educational content. Avoid rigid checkerboard layouts.

## Stories
Use daily life to humanize the brand: work, gym, books, food, polls, questions, project progress, screen recordings, cultural moments.

## Rebrand success test
A visitor should quickly understand:
- Who is Mezie?
- What does he build?
- What will I learn?
- Why should I follow?
