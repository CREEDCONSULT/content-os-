# 3. Platform-Specific Brand Playbook

## Instagram: Identity and discovery

### Role
Show who Mezie is, what he is building, and how he thinks.

### Best content
Reels, Builder Walks, carousels, project proof, lifestyle, fitness, visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes.

### Rhythm
- 3 Reels weekly
- 1 carousel weekly
- Stories 4–6 days weekly
- 1 live or Q&A monthly

### Pinned posts
1. Who I Am
2. What I Am Building
3. What This Page Helps You Do

### Highlights
Start Here, About, Building, AI, Markets, Books, Fitness, Creed, Culture.

## LinkedIn: Authority and opportunity

### Role
Professional credibility, consulting, thought leadership, speaking, case studies.

### Best content
AI and business systems, founder stories, project breakdowns, Africa and diaspora essays, leadership, product strategy.

### Rhythm
- 2 posts weekly
- 1 substantial article monthly
- 10 meaningful comments weekly

## X: Idea laboratory

### Role
Test ideas, sharpen language, join relevant conversations.

### Best content
Short frameworks, market observations, AI commentary, founder notes, threads.

### Rhythm
- 1–3 posts daily
- 1 thread weekly

### Boundary
Do not become outrage-driven or reactive.

## YouTube: Depth and searchable authority

### Role
Teach deeply, document projects, and build long-term trust.

### Best content
AI workflows, project breakdowns, founder documentaries, market education, interviews, tutorials.

### Rhythm
- 2 long-form videos monthly
- 2–4 Shorts weekly from existing material

## TikTok: Reach and experimentation

### Role
Test hooks, simplified explainers, and short-form creative formats.

### Rhythm
Repurpose strongest Reels, but adapt openings and pacing.

## Website: Canonical home

### Pages
Home, About, Projects, Essays, Speaking, Consulting, Media, Newsletter, Contact.

## Newsletter: Owned relationship

### Name options
- Builder Intelligence
- The Builder's Brief
- Building the Future
- Creed Notes

### Rhythm
Weekly or biweekly.

## Cross-platform principle
Use one core message adapted to platform behaviour. Do not publish identical copy everywhere without adjustment.
