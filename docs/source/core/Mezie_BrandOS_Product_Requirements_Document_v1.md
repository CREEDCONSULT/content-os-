# 1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE

This Product Requirements Document defines what Mezie Brand OS must do, who it serves, how its major workflows behave, what belongs in the MVP, what is deferred, and how product success will be measured.

The product is intended to operationalize the existing Mr. C. Mezie brand system, including:

- Founder Identity OS
- Personal Brand Positioning Architecture
- Audience Persona System
- Content Pillars and Series
- Platform Playbooks
- Instagram Rebrand Plan
- LinkedIn Rewrite
- Visual Identity
- Verbal Identity
- Founder Story Library
- August–December Content Operating System
- Proof-of-Work Roadmap
- Autonomous Intelligence and Heartbeat Architecture
- UI/UX Frontend Plan
- BrandOS Skill Library

The product shall not be a generic content scheduler. It shall be a living operating system that understands the founder, preserves brand context, supports content creation, coordinates production, maintains memory, monitors creators and trends, learns from performance, and creates a structured record of brand development.

# 3. PROBLEM STATEMENT

The current personal-brand workflow is fragmented across:

- Chat sessions
- Markdown files
- Obsidian
- Telegram
- Saved Instagram content
- Browser bookmarks
- Voice notes
- Images
- Videos
- Platform analytics
- Project folders
- AI tools
- Editing software
- Personal memory

This creates recurring problems:

1. Brand context is lost between sessions.
2. Ideas are captured but not converted into briefs or scripts.
3. Creator references are saved but not systematically analyzed.
4. Scripts are generated without complete founder context.
5. Production requirements are disconnected from scripts.
6. Content status is difficult to track.
7. Performance data is not converted into reusable learning.
8. Proof of work is not systematically documented.
9. AI tools operate reactively rather than through governed workflows.
10. Personal-brand execution depends too much on founder memory and motivation.

Mezie Brand OS must convert the brand from a collection of documents and ideas into an executable system.

# 8. PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

## 8.1 Context Before Generation

The system shall not generate important content from a blank prompt when relevant brand context exists.

## 8.2 One Source of Truth

Approved dashboard records and canonical vault documents shall be authoritative.

## 8.3 Human Approval Before Public Action

The MVP shall require explicit approval before publishing, external outreach, paid tool usage above budget, canonical strategy changes, or use of sensitive founder stories.

## 8.4 Evidence Before Imitation

The system may study creator mechanics but shall not reproduce another creator’s identity, original wording, or protected creative expression.

## 8.5 Skills Before Agent Sprawl

The MVP shall use one Brand Director agent with specialized skills.

## 8.6 Transparent AI

The user shall be able to see:

- Model used
- Skills used
- Context loaded
- Tools used
- Estimated cost
- Proposed actions
- Memory writes
- Approval requirements
- Confidence
- Errors

## 8.7 Mobile Capture, Desktop Depth

Mobile prioritizes capture and approval. Desktop prioritizes strategy, editing, and analysis.

## 8.8 Learn Through Evidence

The system shall distinguish raw observations, hypotheses, repeated patterns, tested insights, approved learnings, and canonical principles.

# 20. RISKS AND MITIGATIONS

## Overengineering

**Risk:** The system becomes too complex before it becomes useful.  
**Mitigation:** Deliver vertical workflows first.

## Context Overload

**Risk:** Models receive excessive documents.  
**Mitigation:** Use Context Pack Builder and retrieval.

## Generic AI Output

**Risk:** Content sounds like general AI writing.  
**Mitigation:** Require founder evidence, voice, stories, and brand context.

## Copyright and Imitation

**Risk:** Benchmarking becomes copying.  
**Mitigation:** Extract mechanics and require original adaptation.

## Tool Cost

**Risk:** Heartbeat and media research become expensive.  
**Mitigation:** Budgets, caching, watch tiers, model routing.

## Sensitive Financial Content

**Risk:** Brand appears to give investment signals.  
**Mitigation:** Financial Content Safety skill and approval gate.

## Data Fragmentation

**Risk:** Database and vault diverge.  
**Mitigation:** Defined source-of-truth rules and reconciliation jobs.

## Dependence on Platform APIs

**Risk:** API restrictions block analytics or publishing.  
**Mitigation:** Manual and CSV workflows remain supported.
